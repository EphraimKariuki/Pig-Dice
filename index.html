These are the rules of the game

  1. if the player rolls a 1, they score nothing
    and the other palyer is to play
  2. if a play rolls any other number it is added to their turn total
    and player's turn continues
  3. if a player chooses to "hold" the turn total is added to their score
    and it becomes the next players turn
  4. the game ends when either of the party score 100
    and the player becomes the Winner
